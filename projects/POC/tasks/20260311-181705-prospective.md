**Outcome:** Did not materialize — files existed but weren't in the right place or written at the right time
**Learning:** The pre-mortem defensive posture on edge cases (permissions, symlinks, encoding) was a false alarm. When sequencing and path risks are present, they mask file-state edge cases. The actual bug was not in the check's edge-case handling, but in *when* and *where* files were placed.
