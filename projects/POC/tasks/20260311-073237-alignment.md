## [2026-03-11] Alignment Observation
**Category:** Boundary Deviation  
**Signal:** Intent listed escalation triggers: "Sessions at PLANNING_ESCALATE need special handling" and "Stream files incomplete/truncated — flag whether TUI should surface this before resuming." Neither was addressed or flagged in the implementation. Error/crash states (non-zero exit, hard kill) also not explicitly handled.  
**Implication:** The implementation covers "orphaned at approval gates" well but skips the error/escalation surface. If these states are rare, the scope might be correct — but if reboots commonly land sessions in PLANNING_ESCALATE or error states, the fix is incomplete. Next iteration should enumerate: how often do each state types occur in real reboots, and which are safe to ignore?
