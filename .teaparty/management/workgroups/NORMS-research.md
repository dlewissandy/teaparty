# Research Workgroup Norms

## Scope

- Research retrieves and summarizes — it does not produce original content or perform deep analysis
- Work is complete when the brief is answered with sourced evidence, not when all possible sources are exhausted

## Source quality

- Prefer primary sources over secondary
- Note source quality in findings: peer-reviewed vs. grey literature, recency, sample size
- Flag conflicting findings explicitly; resolution is the requestor's call

## Handoff

- Write findings to scratch using the digest skill before reporting completion
- Findings must be attributed — every claim needs a source URL or citation
