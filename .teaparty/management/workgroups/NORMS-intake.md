# Intake Workgroup Norms

## Scope

- Intake clarifies what is wanted — dispatch when even the approach is unknown
- Intake is upstream of planning; dispatch planning after intake produces an approved requirements artifact

## Process

- Escalate early and often — the default is to ask, not assume
- Requirements artifact must be approved by the requestor before intake declares completion

## Output

- Deliverable is a requirements artifact that planning and execution teams can act on directly
- Every statement in the artifact must be actionable — vague requirements are a bug
