# Planning Workgroup Norms

## Scope

- Planning decides how work should be done — not who does it or whether it should be done
- Dispatch before execution when scope or approach is uncertain; dispatch again after a backtrack

## Process

- Constraints, priorities, and success criteria must be defined before meaningful planning can begin
- Lead synthesizes specialist outputs — strategy, risk register, milestones, dependencies — into one coherent plan

## Output

- Plans must cover scope, sequencing, risks, and dependencies at a level of detail sufficient to begin execution
