# Writing Workgroup Norms

## Scope

- Writing produces original content from a brief — research must be complete before dispatch
- Do not send existing content here to be improved; that belongs to editorial

## Quality

- Content must match the format, audience, and scope defined in the brief
- Writers request clarification rather than assuming on undefined brief elements

## Handoff

- Write drafts to scratch using the digest skill; lead stitches and delivers
